Services

Practical, high-impact supervision packages.

Clinical Supervision

Individual or group supervision for social workers, counsellors, and play therapists delivering child and family support across Calgary and area.

  • Reflective practice and case conceptualization
  • Risk mitigation, documentation, and ethics coaching
  • Developmental planning tailored to professional goals

Program Consultation

Strategic guidance to align programming with emerging research, funder expectations, and the lived realities of the families you serve.

  • Policy review and compliance readiness
  • Outcome measurement and reporting support
  • Trauma-informed staff training and onboarding

Emergency Case Support

On-call consultation for crisis scenarios requiring immediate direction, safety planning, and coordination with partner agencies.

  • Access to an experienced supervisor within 24 hours
  • Collaborative safety and escalation planning
  • Follow-up debrief to care for your team
